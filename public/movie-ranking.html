Books I've Read

Tiny summary with detailed notes for each (eventually). This page will constantly update as I read more, so bookmark it if you want to check back in a few months

You Can Negotaite Aything by Herb Cohen

Everything is negotiable. Challenge authority. You have the power in any situation. A must read classic from 1980 from a master negotaitor. This little book is filled with so may memoriable stories - examples of great day to day moments of negotiation that will stick in your head for when you need them.


The Listening Book by W.A. Mathieu

Everyone should read this book of essays about listening. It teaches your ears to pay more attention. It calls your attention to sounds you hadn't noticed. It's beautifully written and makes your life better


The Courage To Be Disliked by Ichiro Kishimi and Fumitake Koga

Wow. A profound little philosopy book from Japan, communicating the psychology of Alfred Adler - a rival of Freud. Told as a conversation between an angry student and a patient teacher. A suprisingly fresh perspective on how to live.
